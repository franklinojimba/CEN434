The Power of a Simple To-Do List

Posted on October 26, 2025 by Franklin

Sometimes, the easiest tools are the best. I used to rely on fancy apps, but lately, a simple handwritten to-do list has changed my life. There's something very satisfying about crossing off a task with a pen. It helps me focus and not get distracted by all the other things my computer and phone try to show me. I think this simplicity is a principle I want to bring into my coding projects too. Less complexity, more focus on the main goal.

Favorite Recipe of the Week: Lemon Pasta

Posted on October 22, 2025 by Franklin

If you need a quick, bright meal, you have to try this recipe. It’s just pasta, lemon juice, zest, butter, and a bit of parmesan. It sounds simple, but the flavor is incredible and it takes less than 20 minutes to make from start to finish. I always add a pinch of red pepper flakes for a tiny bit of heat.
